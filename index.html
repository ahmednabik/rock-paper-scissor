Rules of the Game

How to Play: The game follows the traditional rules of Rock, Paper, Scissors - Rock crushes Scissors, Scissors cut Paper, and Paper wraps Rock.

Number of Rounds: The game consists of a maximum of five rounds.

Winning a Round: A round is won by the player whose selection beats the other player's selection, according to the traditional rules of Rock, Paper, Scissors.

Tied Rounds: In the case of a tie (both players select the same thing), the round will be replayed until a decisive outcome is achieved.

Winning the Game: The first player to win three rounds out of five is declared the "Ultimate RPS Champion.
